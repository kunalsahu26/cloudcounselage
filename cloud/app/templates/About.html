Cloud Counselage Pvt. Ltd. is an IT & Management Consulting & Services company with a “Social Cause” at heart. We serve startups and MSMEs. Our certified professionals have catered to industries like Technology, FMCG, Fintech, Healthcare, Training, Professional Services, etc.
The ‘Social Cause’ is to eradicate perpetual unemployability issue created due to lack of Industry Exposure & Experience, and Job-Readiness amongst the freshers from Tech., Mgmt. and other backgrounds. The delayed influx of these critical human resources from the ‘academia’ into the ‘industry’ causes loss of crucial working hours, in-turn causing a great loss to the Indian economy. We intend to provide 360 degree professional development platform to the students, making them capable to take up IT/ ITes & Mgmt. roles.
